Avinash Prabhakaran

Education

I was born in the idyllic state of Kerala in India. I started my schooling in Kendriya Vidyalaya and moved across different states in the country with my parents, both managers in public sector companies. Then we moved to Jersey, UK where I finished my schooling from Le Rocquier School and Hautlieu School. After school, I went back to India to my home state to do my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Federal Institute of Science and Technology under Mahatma Gandhi University. Bitten by the bug of AI during my engineering years, I flew to Vancouver to do my Masters in Data Science at The University of British Columbia, Vancouver. I look forward to become a Data Scientist, and a good one at that.


Employment

During my final year of Engineering I got campus placement with TCS and Standard Chartered GBS. After graduation, I took up the offer of Standard Chartered GBS, Chennai as an Analyst. I worked as part of Identity and Access Management (IAM) & Global Information Security (GIS) teams, which integrate business-critical applications to Oracle Identity Governance and SailPoint IdentityIQ, to automate user restrictions and prevent data leakage. I worked there for a year till I called it quits in July 2017 to pursue my real passion in the fields of Data Science and Machine Learning.

After I completed masters degree in Data Science from The University of British Columbia, I joined Canvass Analytics, a global leader in AI industrial advanced analytics in Sept 2018. The role requires the skills of a data scientist and machine learning engineering to use AI for intelligent industrial operations. So far I have dealt with industries in oil and gas, steel, and forest-produce sectors.

You are welcome to find details of these projects in the Blogs and Projects section of my Website. I have also done many projects on Data Science and Machine Learning which can be found on my GitHub page.
